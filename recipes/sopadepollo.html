Sopa de Pollo

The classic chicken soup consists of a clear chicken broth, often with pieces of chicken or vegetables;
common additions are pasta, noodles, dumplings, or carrots, and grains such as rice and barley.
Chicken soup is commonly considered a comfort food.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ quarts chicken stock
  • 2 pounds skin-on, bone-in whlie chicken legs (drumsticks and thighs)
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 avocado, peeled and chopped
  • 2 serrano peppers, minced
  • 2 limes, quartered

Directions

  1. Bring stock to a boil in a large pot over high heat. Add chicken legs; return to a boil.
    Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, skimming away foam that forms on the surface.
    Add carrots, onion, celery, cilantro, garlic, and mint.

  2. Cover pot and cook until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear,
    about 30 minutes more. Remove chicken from the pot; discard skin and bones. Shred meat using 2 forks.

  3. Return chicken meat to the pot and season with salt and pepper. Add rice. Let chicken and rice warm up, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and pour soup into serving bowls. Top with avocado and serrano peppers and squeeze in lime juice.
